Personal Data Policy

At rolandades.com, we are fully committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users. This Personal Data Policy outlines the principles we follow when collecting, handling, and safeguarding your personal information. We believe in transparency, minimal data collection, and absolute respect for your rights.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We understand the importance of your personal information and take our responsibility to protect it seriously. Our core principles include:

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.

  • We only collect data that is necessary, relevant, and proportionate to the services we provide.

  • We store your information securely and limit access to authorised personnel only.

  • We give you full control over your data, including access, correction, and deletion rights.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data only when voluntarily submitted by you:

  • Name and email address (e.g. via contact forms or newsletter subscriptions)

  • Communication content if you contact us directly

  • IP address and device/browser information for analytics and security purposes

  • User preferences for cookies, if accepted

We do not collect sensitive data such as passport numbers, payment details, or health information.

3. How We Use Personal Data

Your personal data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your enquiries

  • Sending newsletters (only if you opt in)

  • Improving website performance and user experience

  • Ensuring website security and functionality

  • Meeting legal obligations or defending legal claims if required

We do not use personal data for profiling or automated decision-making.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal data to any third party for commercial gain.

We may use carefully selected service providers (e.g., for hosting or email delivery), all of whom are contractually required to:

  • Handle your data securely

  • Act only on our instructions

  • Never share or reuse your data

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected — or as required by applicable law. For example:

  • Contact form submissions: up to 12 months

  • Newsletter subscriptions: until you unsubscribe

  • Analytics data: anonymised and retained as per industry standards

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data

  • Ask us to correct or delete your data

  • Withdraw your consent at any time

  • Object to the processing of your data

  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

📩 privacy@rolandades.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to understand how visitors use our content. Cookie use is clearly disclosed, and visitors can manage preferences or opt out. No personal data is collected without consent.

For more details, see our full Privacy Policy.

8. International Compliance

Our data handling practices are designed to comply with:

  • New Zealand Privacy Act 2020

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU/EEA visitors

  • Industry best practices for ethical and transparent data handling

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Personal Data Policy periodically to reflect changes in legislation or operational practices. All updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
